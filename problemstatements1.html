Theme 2 : logistics and Management

1. Chat Bot

Develop a chat bot designed to enhance the user experience for both producers and customers.

Chat Bot

Objective: Develop a chat bot designed to enhance the user experience for both producers and customers.


Summary: This chat bot should use intelligent features such as avatars or voice-based systems to provide a more engaging and effective interface. The goal is to streamline interactions, address queries, and offer recommendations, ultimately contributing to improved business outcomes.

2. Demand Forecasting and Comparison

Create a system that predicts future product demand and compares current prices to offer optimal suggestions to customers.

Demand Forecasting and Comparison

Objective:Create a system that predicts future product demand and compares current prices to offer optimal suggestions to customers.


Summary: The system should utilize advanced data analysis and forecasting techniques to anticipate market trends, helping businesses plan inventory and pricing strategies more effectively. This will enable better decision-making and customer satisfaction through informed product recommendations.

3. Transportation and Route Planning

Optimize transportation routes and logistics to minimize costs and delivery times.

Transportation and Route Planning

Objective:Optimize transportation routes and logistics to minimize costs and delivery times.


Details: Implement solutions using real-time traffic data or simulated annealing algorithms to enhance route efficiency. This will reduce operational costs and improve delivery speed, ensuring timely and cost-effective transportation of goods.

4. Digital Tipping for Wait Staff

Develop a comprehensive online tipping solution.

Digital Tipping for Wait Staff

Objective:Develop a comprehensive online tipping solution.


Summary: The solution should seamlessly integrate with existing digital platforms to allow easy and secure tipping for wait staff. This will modernize the tipping process, improve user convenience, and ensure fair and efficient distribution of tips.

5. Vehicle Booking Systems

Develop an advanced vehicle booking system.

Vehicle Booking Systems

Objective:Develop an advanced vehicle booking system.


Summary: This system should feature real-time availability tracking, efficient resource management, and cost control. It aims to streamline the booking process, enhance fleet utilization, and provide accurate tracking for users.

6. Patient Medicine Distribution and Management

Enhance the management of patient and medicine data.

Patient Medicine Distribution and Management

Objective:Enhance the management of patient and medicine data.


Summary: Focus on improving the quality of care, treatment scheduling, and real-time management of diagnoses and medication. This system should support better coordination of healthcare services, leading to improved patient outcomes and operational efficiency in healthcare facilities.

7. Assisting Farmers with Meteorological Data

Create a solution that helps farmers by providing timely updates and warnings about weather conditions.

Assisting Farmers with Meteorological Data

Objective: To create a solution that helps farmers by providing timely updates and warnings about weather conditions that may affect them.


Summary: Updating and warning the farmers about the weather in the coming days can help them stay alert. The product should be user-friendly and accessible to the farmers.

8. AI-Assisted Distracted Driver Detection System

Design and implement a system that uses AI algorithms to detect distracted driving behaviors in real-time.

AI-Assisted Distracted Driver Detection System

Objective: To design and implement a system that uses AI algorithms to detect distracted driving behaviours in real-time.


Summary: The system should analyse cues such as facial expressions, eye movements, head orientation, and vehicle dynamics to identify signs of distraction, drowsiness, or inattention on the part of the driver

9. Decentralized File Storage

Decentralized File Storage

Build a web3 application that utilises decentralised storage systems like IPFS or Filecoin to enable secure and censorship-resistant file storage and sharing.

10. Blockchain-based Digital Identity Verification

Develop a blockchain solution for secure and efficient identity verification, reducing reliance on traditional methods.

Blockchain-based Digital Identity Verification

Develop a blockchain solution that allows individuals or organizations to verify identities securely and efficiently, reducing reliance on traditional identity verification methods.

Open Source
