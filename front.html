INFOTHON 2.0

Welcome to Infothon 2024, the ultimate offline hackathon hosted by the Information Science and Engineering Department of VVCE Mysore! Infothon 2024 is an exhilarating 24-hour coding extravaganza that brings together passionate innovators, programmers, and problem solvers from all walks of life. Held at the prestigious VVCE campus in Mysore, this hackathon is designed to ignite creativity, foster collaboration, and push the boundaries of technological innovation. Join us at Infothon 2024, where creativity, collaboration, and coding excellence come together to shape the future. Get ready to embark on an unforgettable hackathon experience that will challenge, inspire, and empower you to create something extraordinary. Let's code for a brighter tomorrow!
